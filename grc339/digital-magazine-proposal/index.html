Introduction

Hoa Mai

Hoa Mai is a digital magazine inspired by the rich cultural heritage of Vietnam. Drawing inspiration from the symbolic hoa mai flower, an emblem of spring, renewal, and prosperity in Vietnamese tradition, this publication focuses on the vibrant city of Saigon and embodies aspects of life, fashion, and food that highlights the Vietnamese experience.
